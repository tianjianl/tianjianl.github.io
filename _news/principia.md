Our new work: [Reasoning over mathematical objects: on-policy reward modeling and test time aggregation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.18886) is out! In this work we 1) built and released training data for deriving mathematical objects; 2) show that on-policy RL with strong verifier boosts performance, and 3) on-policy training on parallel generation + verification further boosts the performance.
